Red or Blue
A game of Trust

How to play
This game is to be played with a friend. You should guess red or blue, your friend can tell you the truth or lie about the correct answer (answers are in the Docs file given at the end of this guide).

There are a total of 3 rounds to guess. Guess them all to win! Good luck.
